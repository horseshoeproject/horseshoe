About

The inspiration for Horseshoe came from a desire to make the job of “finding a job” easier. It is designed to function as an organized database of applications and a to-do list that encourages best practices for jobseekers, allowing you to use your time most effectively and most efficiently. Get the job you deserve with Horseshoe.
